Your place for all Intersect-related polling. All polls on this platform
are powered by Ekklesia but run by
[Intersect](https://intersectmbo.org).

You can look up any DRep voting on this tool in the
[Voter Directory](/voter-directory). The directory is public, refreshes
every 10 minutes, and votes can change until a ballot closes. Final
results may differ slightly from preliminary tallies as DRep voting
power may change during the vote — final results are published on-chain
and can be independently verified.
