Verifiable, Hydra-powered on-chain voting on Cardano. Browse upcoming and
live ballots below, or look up any active voter in the
[Voter Directory](/voter-directory). The directory is public, refreshes
every 10 minutes, and votes can change until a ballot closes — final
results are published on-chain and can be independently verified.
