Ekklesia is a voting tool for verifiable, transparent on-chain voting on
Cardano, developed by [Adam Dean](https://x.com/adamkdean) and
[Mad Orkestra](https://bsky.app/profile/madorkestra.com). It was initially
funded by [IOG](https://x.com/InputOutputHK) and
[Intersect](https://intersectmbo.org), and continues development through
[Project Catalyst Fund 14](https://projectcatalyst.io/funds/14/cardano-use-cases-concepts/ekklesia-high-frequency-voting-on-cardano-with-hydra).

Ekklesia accommodates votes for every kind of voter group — DReps, SPOs, token
and NFT holders, or anyone with a Cardano wallet. It supports MultiSigs as
well as CLI-based authentication via the CardanoSigner.

Read more about how Ekklesia works at
[docs.ekklesia.vote](https://docs.ekklesia.vote).
