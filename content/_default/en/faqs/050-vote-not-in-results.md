---
question: "I voted but my vote doesn't seem to be included in the results"
order: 50
---

For performance reasons, provisional results are rolled up roughly every 10
minutes. If you just submitted and signed your votes, it may take a few
minutes for them to appear in the tally.

Always make sure you have signed and submitted every vote, and that no votes
remain marked as unsubmitted on your dashboard.

Once a ballot closes the numbers may also shift when the voting authority
applies their eligibility and voting-power weighting — see "Final results
deviate slightly..." below for more.
